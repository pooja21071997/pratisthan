About Our Institution

Keshav Smruti Pratishthan's unique education, inspired by the ancient Gurukul system, focuses on building disciplined, physically fit citizens with strong character beyond academics. Established in 1987, Vivekanand Pratishthan blends culture with scientific education.

Our Values

Man-Making Education

Education is an effective medium of Insisting on quality in every subject man-making and character building.

Continuous Learning

Embracing evolving teaching methods and continuous learning shapes a stronger personality.

Responsibility and Accountability

A person must fulfill their personal, social, national, and human duties.

Qualitative education

Continuous study builds quality education by adding values and skills.

Nationalism and Patriotism

Patriotism is augmented by offering mind, body and life in the service of the motherland.

Social Sensitivity

Providing quality education for all,and activity based learning for the deprived is our duty.

Striving for excellence

Insisting on quality in every subject and implementing activities accordingly.

Team Work

Work with respect for others values, guided by elders ideas, and serve socienty with excellence.
