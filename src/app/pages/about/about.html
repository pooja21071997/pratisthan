Vivekanand Pratishthan

Keshav Smruti Pratishthan follows a holistic education model inspired by the ancient Gurukul system.

We focus on developing knowledge, discipline, physical fitness, and strong values, not just academic memory.

Established in 1987, Vivekanand Pratishthan blends education and culture with scientific thinking.

Our 10-acre campus, located 7 km from the city, serves students from Grade V to X, with modern facilities, transport, and hostel support.

With 2,200 students and 150 dedicated staff members, we nurture future-ready citizens.

Beyond education, the institution runs 11 social and community initiatives, contributing meaningfully to society.
